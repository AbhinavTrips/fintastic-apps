Independent Android publisher & first-party marketing platform

What Fintastic Apps does

Fintastic Apps is the marketing-automation platform we built and operate for our own business. Its purpose is to help us grow and monetize our own Android apps responsibly. It connects, using our own authorized credentials, to the advertising and analytics services we already use — Google Ads, Google Analytics, Google AdMob, and Meta Ads — to (1) read performance data for our own ad campaigns and apps (spend, installs, clicks, conversions, active users, revenue) and (2) create and manage our own app-install advertising campaigns. It is an internal tool for Fintastic Apps only; it is not sold, and it does not process data on behalf of any third party. Our use of Google APIs complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Responsible use & safeguards

Because our platform can spend advertising budget, every action runs under human-approved safeguards: dry-run by default, new campaigns created paused, approval gates for budget increases, hard daily spend caps, and an instant kill switch. We access only our own accounts and do not sell this tooling or process data for third parties.
