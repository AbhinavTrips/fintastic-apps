Fintastic Apps

Fintastic Apps is an in-house marketing-automation platform that we, an independent Android app publisher, use to advertise and grow our own mobile apps. It connects to our advertising and analytics accounts to report on and manage our own ad campaigns.

First-party marketing automation for our own apps

What this application does

Fintastic Apps is the marketing-automation application we built and operate solely for our own business. Its purpose is to help us advertise, grow, and monetize our own Android apps. Using our own authorized accounts, it connects to the advertising and analytics services we already use — Google Ads, Google Analytics, Google AdMob, and Meta Ads — and provides two capabilities:

  • ReportingReads performance data for our own ad campaigns and apps — advertising spend, impressions, clicks, app installs, conversions, active users, and revenue — and combines it into dashboards so we can measure results.
  • Campaign managementCreates and manages our own app-install advertising campaigns (budgets, status, and settings) based on that performance data.

It is an internal tool used only by Fintastic Apps. It is not sold or made available to the public, and it does not process data on behalf of any third party.

Data we access and why

When you sign in with Google, Fintastic Apps requests access to the Google Ads account you authorize (OAuth scope https://www.googleapis.com/auth/adwords). We use this access only to (1) read reporting data for the authorizing account's own advertising campaigns and (2) create and manage that account's own campaigns, as described above. We access only accounts we own and operate. We do not use Google user data for advertising to unrelated parties, we do not sell it, and we do not transfer it to third parties except as needed to operate these internal workflows. Our use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including its Limited Use requirements. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

Responsible use & safeguards

Because our platform can spend advertising budget, every action runs under human-approved safeguards: dry-run by default, new campaigns created paused, approval gates for budget increases, hard daily spend caps, and an instant kill switch. We access only our own accounts and do not sell this tooling or process data for third parties.
