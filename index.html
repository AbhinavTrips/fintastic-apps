How we grow responsibly

We operate our own first-party marketing automation to understand app performance and improve return on our advertising. It reads analytics and campaign data from the platforms we already use (Google Analytics, Google AdMob, Google Ads, and others) strictly for our own apps, and manages our own advertising under human-approved safeguards — spend caps, approval gates, and dry-run by default. We do not sell this tooling or process data for third parties.
