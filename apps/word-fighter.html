Word Fighter

Place. Swipe. Score.

A fast, competitive word game. Place letters on a shared board, swipe to claim words, and lock cells in your color — grab the bonus tiles before your opponent does.

Visit wordfighterapp.pro

How you play

  • Place your lettersEach player places letters on their turn to build the board.
  • Swipe to claimSpot a word and swipe across the cells to claim it for points.
  • Lock the boardClaimed words lock cells in your color — deny your rival space.
  • Bonus cellsA Triple Word cell on a 6-letter word can be devastating.
